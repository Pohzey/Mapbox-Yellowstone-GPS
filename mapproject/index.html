Must See Landmarks and Wildlife Spots in Yellowstone National Park

The Yellowstone National Park one of the most intact ecosystems remaining in North America and is largely considered the first National Park in the world. Yellowstone is one of the worlds active supervolcanos which has erupted several times in the past couple million years, each time being a globe altering event. Due to this, Yellowstone is an incredibly active geothermal region where one of the most famous landmarks is the Old Faithful Geyser. Wildlife has incredible diversity here, where you could observe the largest public Bison herd in the world and observe wild animals in their most natural habitats. This map will pinpoint some of the many beautiful and most popular stopping points to visit during any trip made to Yellowstone National Park.
